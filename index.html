Shota Ekuni (江國翔太)

@ekunish

I am Ph. D. candidate at University of Tsukuba(Japan), and specialized in Cybernics, a frontier science that centers on cybernetics, mechatronics, and informatics, and developed various engineering systems; such as robots, nano-satellites, and wearable devices. My research for my doctorate focuses on the non-invasive measurement for Hematocrit, a volume ratio of red blood cells in the blood. To achieve this research, I have constructed a world-first measurement theory by using an optical method and developed a new sensor for the theory.
